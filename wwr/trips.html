Super Zipline - Brotas

Can you imagine flying 150 meters high like a superhero? That's the feeling of the Super Zipline! In this activity you will fly on two zip lines, the first with 1.1 km in length and the second with 800 meters. As a gift, our customers get a *2km trail through dense forest and a visit to a waterfall.

Cassorova Eco park: panoramic adventure

Explore the lush greenery of the Cassorova Eco Park with a full-day admission ticket. The park is home to two of Brazil’s most beautiful waterfalls: Cassorova and Quatis which are 197 feet (60 meters) and 150 feet (46 meters) in height respectively. Walk through the lush jungle, swim in the natural pools of the waterfalls, and enjoy panoramic views of Brazilian countryside throughout the day.

Singing sand

“Singing Sand” . It refers to a magical place in São Paulo,Brotas, Brazil, where the sand dunes emit musical sounds when you walk on them. This phenomenon occurs due to the special composition of the sand and the way the particles move when pressed. It’s truly a unique experience!
